Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about working with us

How long does it take to build a website?

Most websites are completed within 2-4 weeks from project start. This includes design, development, content integration, and testing. Larger projects may take 4-6 weeks.

What's included in the price?

Everything you need: custom design, development, mobile optimization, SEO setup, SSL certificate, hosting setup, analytics integration, and 30 days of post-launch support. No hidden fees.

Do you provide ongoing maintenance?

Yes! We offer monthly maintenance packages that include updates, backups, security monitoring, and content changes. Most clients choose our maintenance plan for peace of mind.

Can you help with content and copywriting?

Absolutely. We have professional copywriters who specialize in conversion-focused content. We'll help craft messaging that resonates with your audience and drives action.

What if I need changes after launch?

We include 30 days of free revisions after launch. After that, you can either use our maintenance plan or request changes on an hourly basis. We're always here to help your site evolve.
