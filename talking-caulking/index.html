The Dripless Difference

Our patented anti-drip mechanism uses a spring-loaded rod that automatically releases pressure when you stop squeezing. This prevents the backflow that causes messy drips in traditional caulking guns. The result? Clean, professional beads with zero waste.

40 Years of Excellence

From a small workshop in Santa Rosa to the most trusted name in dispensing tools, Dripless has always stood for one thing: precision without compromise.

What started as Harold Dripless's frustration with messy, unreliable caulking guns became a mission to engineer the perfect tool. Today, contractors and DIY enthusiasts trust Dripless for the same reason they always have—because it simply works better.
